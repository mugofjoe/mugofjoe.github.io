New Cases: A Reinvigorated Disease

A Timeline of a Modern-Day Pandemic By Joel Dazo

The number of new coronavirus cases has not dipped below 10,000 since March 23. Most of the states have started re-opening in the latter week of April through May. Perhaps the effect of the re-opening may explain the strong resurgence of new COVID-19 cases beginning in June.
