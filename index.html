A Timeline of a Modern-Day Pandemic

By Joel Dazo

The coronavirus originated from a wet market in Wuhan China in the late 2019. Since then, it has spread to more than 177 countries, taken the lives of more than 600,000 people and made around 14.7 million people ill worldwide prompting the World Health Organization to declare it as a pandemic.
